# Cycles

A private, simple cycle tracking app without dark patterns.

## Core Principles

- **Local-first**: Your data stays on your device by default
- **No trackers**: Zero third-party analytics or ads
- **Honest predictions**: Shows uncertainty, not false precision
- **Your data, your control**: Easy export and complete deletion
